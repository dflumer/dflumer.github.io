friday, december 27th 2024

“it's a dangerous business, frodo, going out your door. You step onto the road, and if you don't keep your feet, there's no knowing where you might be swept off to.” -j.r.r. tolkien

this is exactly how i feel about writing my first post. i have no idea where to start or what to do, but i know the most important part is that i begin. a lot of people roll their eyes at new year resolutions, but i have this feeling, deep down, that 2025 will be my year. because of that, i want to document how my year unfolds. i've got a lot of big goals ahead, and i hope i can reach them—or at least stay on the right path. i feel like, for the last six months, i've been cultivating this mindset of what i want to achieve and now with the start of the new year i'm finally putting pen to paper.
