    about me.

    hi — i’m dana.

    i’m a computational biology graduate, currently working as a business analyst and taking prerequisite courses for a PhD in biomathematics at florida state university. i’m passionate about using math and models to better understand biological systems, especially under extreme conditions. i eventually want to conduct research that bridges biology, space science, and AI.

    outside of work and school, i’m usually in the garden, doing pilates, reading, or playing old-school runescape. i’m also slowly building this website as a creative outlet — part portfolio, part journal, part personal archive.
